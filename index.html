Community Guidelines

Welcome to Community Skill Sharing! To ensure a positive, safe, and productive environment for all learners and teachers, please adhere to the following guidelines:

1. Respect and Inclusivity

  • Treat all members with respect, regardless of their skill level, background, or identity.
  • Do not use hate speech, harassment, or discriminatory language and Maintain professional and courteous communication.

2. Content Standards

  • Only post skills that you are genuinely capable of teaching.
  • Skills must be legal, safe, and appropriate for all ages. Sessions must remain free of charge. This is a community sharing platform, not a marketplace.

3. Safety and Privacy

  • Do not share personal contact information (except for necessary scheduling) or private details of others.
  • For in-person sessions, always meet in a public, safe location.

Failure to comply with these guidelines may result in the removal of your posted skills or access to the platform.
